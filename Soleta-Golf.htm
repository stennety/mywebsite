Soleta Golf Club - Nick Price - Florida

Soleta Golf Club is a new Nick Price designed golf course in the Sarasota/Bradenton/Lakewood Ranch area. Site golf construction is underway and will be ready for play in November 2024.

The three development principals David Turner, John Galt and Charles Duff are eager to open the 7,400 yard, par-72 Soleta Golf Club. The golf course is core golf and independently routed, separate from homesites and amenities. All 18 holes wind through the natural Florida wetlands site with sand and native areas adjacent to play. Turfgrass management will managed to a firm and fast standard. Water conservation is a critical planning issue in conjunction with the enhancement of wildlife habitat and sound environmental stewardship.

Additionally, golf instructor David Leadbetter, PGA, announced his design of a 30 acre practice facility. The outdoor practice areas are complemented by an indoor center including a state-of-the-art biomechanics studio, clubfitting, a putting studio, simulators and other advanced training technologies.
