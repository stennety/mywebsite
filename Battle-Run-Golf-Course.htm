Battle Run Golf Course - Nebraska

Par 5 Partners LLC with Planning & Design firm Olsson have submitted plans for a new golf course, Battle Run Golf Course, in Lincoln, Nebraska. Par 5 Partners' Peed family have also created the Dormie Network which owns several golf courses nationwide.

Plans for the property have lodging for members and the golf course in two paddocks with groundwater wells tied into the pumphouse and irrigation system.
