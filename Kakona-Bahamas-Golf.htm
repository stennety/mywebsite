Kakona Resort - Golf Resort in the Bahamas

Kakona Resort is a proposed luxury resort destination in the Bahamas and is poised to become a one of a kind oasis focused on luxury, longevity, and legacy. The Grand Ana Resort & Spa at High Bank Bay on the island of Great Abaco is the location of Kakona. Guests will experience an 18 hole oceanfront golf course designed by David McLay Kidd. Kidd is a renowned golf course architect known for his work on courses such as Bandon Dunes in Oregon, Nanea in Hawaii, and the Castle Course at St Andrews in Scotland.
