Pine Ranch Golf: Scheffler + Chet Williams

Golf architect Chet Williams and PGA Tour Scottie Scheffler are teaming up to create a new golf course at Pine Ranch east of Dallas.

After reading George Thomas' book on golf architecture, Scheffler became interested in design and aiding Williams with planning the new golf course.
