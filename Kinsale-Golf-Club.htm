Kinsale Golf Club in Naples, Florida - Hanse Wagner

Kinsale Golf Club in Naples Florida, designed by Gil Hanse with Jim Wagner, is a notable addition to the world of golf course architecture. In the October 2023 update posted to Vimeo.com, architect Jim Wagner presents the site under construction and details the requirements to raise the site off the base plain. Director of agronomy for the grow-in and opening of the golf course in November 2024 was Rusty Mercer. Mercer led construction of the 18 inch sandcap across the property.

Anthony Soave is the owner and CEO of Kinsale Golf Club. Gil Hanse is known for his design philosophy that emphasizes natural and strategic golf courses. His work often features minimalist movement of terrain, close walkable routings with a blend of challenge and playability for a wide audience of golfers. From the design plans, it is apparent that Kinsale is inspired by Charles Blair Macdonald and Seth Raynor template holes.

Hanse has been involved in the design of many notable courses around the world, including the Olympic Golf Course in Rio de Janeiro for the 2016 Olympics, Les Bordes in France, Boston Golf Club, Rustic Canyon in California, and Ohoopee Match Club in Georgia. Hanse Golf Course Design recently designed and built the West Course at The Apogee Club in Stuart, Florida.

Over the years, the Lion's Mouth bunker has been adapted by various golf course architects. The Coore Crenshaw version on the 8th hole at Sand Hills in Mullen, Nebraska is the most cited modern implementation. Modern renditions of the bunker obstacle do not always strictly adhere to the traditional circular shape but do maintain the core concept of a central obstacle or raised central mound within the bunker fronting the green.

Harrison Hepner, PGA was the Head Golf Professional of Kinsale Golf Club at opening in November 2024.
