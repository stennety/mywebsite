Great Keppel Island is a beautiful and unspoiled island located off the coast of Queensland, Australia. The island is home to pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and abundant marine life. The world will turn to Brisbane, Queensland for The 2032 Olympics.

There is a growing interest in starting over on Great Keppel Island. A number of developers are proposing a new development project on the island, rebuilding a luxury resort with a golf course and marina.

The environmental and social impacts of these projects will need to be carefully considered. However, if done correctly, these projects could help to revitalize Great Keppel Island and create a sustainable tourism destination for future generations.

A golf resort is one of the proposed projects for Great Keppel Island. The resort would be designed to be sustainable and environmentally friendly. It would feature an 18-hole golf course designed by Greg Norman. Greg Norman's golf designs are best in Australia at Ellerston and in Vietnam where Norman serves a diplomatic post as tourism ambassador.
