New Golf Resort in France - Domaine Lavagnac

Domaine Lavagnac will be a luxury golf resort development located in the South of France with an 18 hole golf course designed by Nicklaus Design.

The Domaine Lavagnac golf course will be set in the heart of the province of Languedoc, which is now formally part of the Occitanie region.

The resort is planned as a 5 star hotel with spa and tennis plus other hospitality amenities aiming to attract tourists, families and business travelers.

The project is an investment in the South of France and is expected to create an economic boost with additional housing. The project will promote golf tourism in France and make the country a more attractive destination for international golfers.
