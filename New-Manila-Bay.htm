New Manila Bay Golf Resort

The New Manila Bay golf project is a land reclamation development underway with a golf course and other amenities like the Banyan Tree to be added to Manila, Philippines. The project is being led by Bases Conversion and Development Authority (BCDA) and is budgeted at 10 billion PHP.

The proposed golf course will be located on a reclaimed areas in Manila Bay. In addition to the golf course, the New Manila Bay project will also feature amenities, including a clubhouse, spa, convention center, and hotel. The project is expected to be completed by 2025, generating around 10,000 jobs during construction and 5,000 permanent jobs upon completion.

The New Manila Bay golf project is part of the BCDA's larger plan to redevelop the Manila Bay area into a world-class tourist destination. The project is expected to boost the tourism industry in the Philippines, generating billions of pesos in revenue for the government. The BCDA has said that the project will be environmentally sustainable but paused in 2023 to assess impact. In 2024, President allowed the project to proceed with the understanding of what steps will be taken to minimize its impact on the surrounding area. The project is a major development that is expected to have a significant impact on the Philippines. The project is expected to boost the tourism industry, generate jobs, and improve the economy of the country.
